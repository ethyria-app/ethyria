# Off-Page Authority Plan

Market leadership will not come from on-page SEO alone. This file defines the off-page work required after the technical and content foundation is stable.

## Core Goal

Increase branded trust, topical authority, and relevant backlinks for Ethyria as:

- an AI dream interpretation app
- a private dream journal app
- a psychology-based dream analysis product

## Priority Workstreams

### 1. Branded Entity Consistency

- Use the same product name, description, logo, and URL across all profiles.
- Keep GitHub, social profiles, app listings, and product descriptions aligned.
- Avoid conflicting legacy naming from Dreamzzz-era material.

### 2. App and Product Listings

- Google Play listing when available
- Product Hunt or equivalent launch page
- startup directories and app discovery platforms
- Android app review sites

### 3. Thematic Mentions and Backlinks

Focus on sites and communities connected to:

- dream journaling
- sleep improvement
- psychology and self-reflection
- AI-assisted self-analysis
- quantified self / mood tracking

## Suggested Asset Set

- one clear founder/product story
- one press-style product summary
- one privacy-first product summary
- one AI dream interpretation explainer
- consistent screenshots and social preview assets

## Outreach Targets

- dream and sleep blogs
- journaling communities
- Android app reviewers
- AI tool roundups
- privacy-focused software lists

## Measurement

- branded search growth
- referring domains
- backlinks to the homepage and future supporting pages
- mentions of Ethyria without links that can be converted into links
- ranking improvements for dream interpretation and dream journal clusters

## Constraint

Do not start aggressive outreach before live metadata, schema, and supporting content are stable. Off-page promotion works best once the landing page is strong enough to convert the traffic it earns.
