## Screenshot Strategy

### Format

- 2:3 ratio (1080×1920 or 1242×2208 for iOS)
- Brand colors: Dark bg (#100c1f), cyan accents (#01bfff)
- Short bold headline per screenshot (Poppins Bold, gradient text)

### Screenshot Sequence (Story-Based)

1. **Hero/Hook**: "Dein Traum. KI-analysiert." – App icon + gradient bg
2. **Analysis Flow**: "5 Perspektiven. Ein Traum." – Freud/Jung/Spiritual/Bio/General cards
3. **Journal**: "Dein privates Traumtagebuch" – Journal entry view
4. **Symbols**: "Symbole verstehen mit KI" – Symbol recognition visualization
5. **Stats**: "Muster erkennen. Wachsen." – Statistics dashboard
6. **Community**: "Was träumt die Welt?" – Aether/global trends view
7. **Art**: "Dein Traum als Kunstwerk" – AI-generated dream art
8. **Privacy**: "Deine Daten. Deine Kontrolle." – Encryption shield visual

### A/B Test Plan

- **Test 1**: Icon — Current logo vs. moon/eye variant vs. brain-wave variant
- **Test 2**: Screenshot 1 — "Dein Traum. KI-analysiert." vs. "Was bedeutet dein Traum?"
- **Test 3**: Screenshot order — Analysis-first vs. Journal-first vs. Art-first

## Rating Management

### Trigger Points

- After 3rd dream analysis (user engaged, positive experience likely)
- After achieving first "pattern detected" milestone
- After 7 consecutive days of journaling
- NEVER after a crash, error, or negative sentiment analysis

### Review Prompt Template (German)

> Gefällt dir Ethyria? Dein Feedback hilft uns, die App für alle Träumer besser zu machen.
> [Jetzt bewerten] [Später erinnern] [Nein danke]

### Targets

- **Month 3**: 100 reviews, 4.5+ average
- **Month 6**: 500 reviews, 4.7+ average
- **Month 12**: 2000 reviews, 4.8+ average

### Response Strategy

- Reply to ALL 1-3 star reviews within 24h (German + English)
- Reply to 5-star reviews with personalized thanks (weekly batch)
- Template: "Danke für dein Feedback, [Name]! [Address specific issue]. Wir arbeiten daran – update kommt bald."

## Competitive Positioning

### Key Competitors

- **DreamApp**: Generic dream diary, weak AI, strong install base
- **Lucid**: Lucid dreaming focus, not interpretation
- **Dream Moods**: Dictionary-only, no AI, no journal
- **Dreambook**: Basic symbols, no psychology depth

### Ethyria Differentiators (Use in Store Copy)

1. "5 Analyse-Perspektiven" (no competitor offers this)
2. "Freud + Jung + Islamisch + Spirituell + Biosynchron"
3. "KI-generierte Traumbilder" (unique creative feature)
4. "Verschlüsselt & privat" (trust differentiator)
5. "Made in Austria" (local trust for DACH market)
