FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions About Dream Interpretation

Transparent answers about AI dream interpretation, dream symbols, privacy, Freud vs. Jung, and keeping a digital dream journal.

Is Ethyria really free?

Yes. You can use Ethyria for free. Optional in-app purchases unlock additional premium features like PDF exports and extended analyses.

How safe is my dream data?

Your privacy comes first. Your dream data stays on your device — no cloud required, no tracking.

How does the AI dream analysis work?

Ethyria uses advanced AI to identify symbols, emotional themes, and recurring patterns using psychological approaches based on Jung and Freud.

Which analysis modes does Ethyria offer?

Ethyria offers five analysis perspectives: General Interpretation, Freudian Psychoanalysis, Jungian Analytical Psychology, Spiritual Reflection, and Biosynchronous Analysis.

Can I export my analyses?

Yes. As a premium user, you can export each analysis and your monthly statistics as a professionally designed PDF document.

Which devices is Ethyria available on?

Ethyria is currently available for Android. An iOS version is in development.

What does my dream mean?

Dreams are highly individual. Ethyria analyzes your dream symbols, emotions and narrative structures with AI and interprets them psychologically — based on Freud, Jung or biosynchronous methods. Discover what your dream reveals about your subconscious.

What's the difference between Freud and Jung dream interpretation?

Freud sees dreams as expressions of repressed desires and unconscious conflicts. Jung views them as messages from the collective unconscious and works with archetypes. Ethyria offers both approaches as separate analysis perspectives.

Can AI really interpret dreams?

AI can recognize patterns that the human eye misses — recurring symbols, emotional connections, and narrative structures. Ethyria uses this as a tool for self-reflection, not as a substitute for professional therapeutic guidance.

How do I keep a digital dream journal?

Write down your dream right after waking up — ideally with the Ethyria app. Note symbols, emotions and details. The app analyzes your entries automatically and shows you patterns and recurring themes over weeks.

What do recurring dreams mean?

Recurring dreams often point to unresolved emotional issues or unconscious conflicts. Ethyria automatically detects such patterns and helps you understand the underlying messages from your subconscious.

What are the most common dream symbols?

The most common dream symbols include water (emotions), falling (loss of control), flying (freedom), teeth (anxiety), houses (the self), and being chased (avoidance). Ethyria analyzes these symbols in the context of your personal dream history.
